PACKAGE RULES

Clear support when plans change

Rescheduling with notice is separate from the make-up allowance included in the 12-lesson and 25-lesson packages.

WITH NOTICE

Rescheduling

If a parent gives at least 24 hours’ notice, the lesson is rescheduled and does not use a make-up allowance. The new lesson is subject to teacher and family availability and may not be at the original time.

This applies to every package.

ALWAYS PROTECTED

Teacher or Tarteel House changes

If the teacher or Tarteel House cancels or changes a lesson, it is always rescheduled and never uses the family’s make-up allowance.

The replacement time depends on teacher and family availability.

PACKAGE ALLOWANCE

Make-up lessons

The 6-lesson package has no make-up allowance, so a late-notice or unnotified absence counts as a used lesson. The 12-lesson and 25-lesson packages include up to four make-up lessons, even when a child misses without prior notice. After all four are used, a further late-notice or unnotified absence counts as a used lesson.

These are allowances for missed scheduled lessons, not additional free lessons.
