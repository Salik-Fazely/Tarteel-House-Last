WHY FAMILIES CHOOSE US

Qur’an learning, taught with care

One dedicated teacher

Your child learns with the same teacher every session. The relationship that makes learning possible is built, not rotated.

For children aged 5 to 16

Tarteel House currently teaches girls and boys aged 5 to 16. We do not currently offer adult classes.

Parent check-ins built in

Every two months, we invite parents to a 15-minute progress review. If a parent is unavailable or does not want a meeting, we send a visual progress report through WhatsApp instead.

Flexible around family life

Give at least 24 hours’ notice and we reschedule without using a make-up allowance. Teacher or Tarteel House cancellations are always rescheduled and never use the family’s allowance.
