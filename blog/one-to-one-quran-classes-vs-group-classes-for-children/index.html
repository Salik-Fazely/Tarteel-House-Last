One-to-One Quran Classes vs Group Classes for Children

Both formats can help children learn, but individual attention can make a meaningful difference for many families.

Parents often wonder whether individual or group Qur’an lessons are the better choice.

Group classes can help children learn alongside their peers and enjoy a sense of community. However, one-to-one classes allow the teacher to focus entirely on one student.

With personalized attention, mistakes can be corrected immediately, lessons can be adjusted to the child’s pace, and learning goals can be tailored to individual needs.

Many Qur’an teachers find that children often progress more consistently when lessons are personalized and focused on their specific strengths and challenges.
