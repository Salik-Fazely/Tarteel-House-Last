HOW IT WORKS

Learning that works around your child

One-to-one online Qur’an teaching for children, matched to your child’s level and scheduled around your family’s week.

THE PROCESS

From first contact to confident recitation

Five simple steps — most families are in their first session within a week.

  1. 01

    Book your free trial

    Fill in a short form telling us about your child. No payment, no commitment — just a few details so we can find the right match.

    Takes about two minutes.

  2. 02

    We match your teacher

    We read every request personally and match your child with a suitable teacher based on language, level, learning needs, and scheduling availability.

    We contact you within two days. The team may also respond on weekends.

  3. 03

    Attend your free trial lesson

    A relaxed 40-minute session so your child can meet their teacher and you can see how it feels. No preparation needed.

    Completely free, with no obligation to continue.

  4. 04

    Confirm your package

    If you decide to continue, Tarteel House confirms the suitable package and shares payment instructions or a secure payment link through WhatsApp. Paid lessons are scheduled after payment confirmation.

    See the pricing page for full details.

  5. 05

    Begin the journey

    Regular 40-minute lessons, a teacher who gets to know your child, and a structured progress review every two months.

INSIDE A SESSION

What happens in 40 minutes

Warm-up review

Every session opens by revisiting the previous lesson — short, low-stakes, and confidence-building.

New teaching

New letters, sounds, or ayaat are introduced step by step, with the teacher adjusting pace in real time.

Guided practice

Your child reads aloud while the teacher listens, corrects gently, and reinforces correct pronunciation.

A focus for home

The lesson closes with a clear focus for short practice before the next meeting.

BETWEEN SESSIONS

Progress that continues outside the lesson

HOME PRACTICE

A short, focused routine

We suggest ten to fifteen minutes of reading between lessons. The teacher will tell your child exactly what to focus on — no guesswork for you.

Even two or three minutes daily compounds quickly.

EVERY TWO MONTHS

A structured progress review

Every two months, we invite parents to a 15-minute progress review. If a parent is unavailable or does not want a meeting, we send a visual progress report through WhatsApp instead.

OPEN COMMUNICATION

Questions are always welcome

Reach Tarteel House via WhatsApp or email between lessons. Questions are welcome, and the team will help with the next steps.

YOUR PART

What we ask of you

Very little — just consistency and a quiet ten minutes.

A device with internet

Any phone, tablet, or laptop with a stable connection. We use Zoom or Google Meet.

Ten minutes of home reading

Short daily practice between lessons makes the biggest difference. The teacher will set it.

A quiet space

Somewhere your child can concentrate for the session. A little background noise is fine.

A little patience

Every child learns at their own pace. The teacher will meet your child where they are.
