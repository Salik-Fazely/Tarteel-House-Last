WHAT’S INCLUDED

The care that comes with every session

Every bundle — from Starter to Committed — rests on the same foundation. The only difference is the size of the journey.

One dedicated teacher

Your child learns with the same teacher throughout the bundle. The relationship is built, not rotated.

A steady weekly rhythm

Sessions happen on a consistent day and time each week, with room to adjust when family life requires it.

Progress you can follow

Your child’s teacher shares a short summary after each session so you can follow the journey alongside your child.

A thoughtful start, always

Every family begins with a free trial session before committing to a bundle — so the match is right before anything else is decided.

WHAT LARGER BUNDLES ADD

A deeper journey, thoughtfully supported

The Steady and Committed bundles include everything in Starter, plus a few additions that meaningfully support longer learning.

COMPLIMENTARY

Ethics sessions

Every ten Qur’an sessions includes five short Islamic ethics sessions — age-appropriate lessons on character, akhlaq, and the deeper purpose behind recitation.

Included in Steady (5 sessions) and Committed (10 sessions).

STRUCTURED

Parent check-in calls

A structured 15-minute call between parent and teacher every ten sessions. The teacher walks you through your child’s strengths, focus areas, and the path ahead.

Included in Steady and Committed.

WRITTEN

Progress summaries

For families on the Committed bundle, periodic written summaries of what your child has learned and how the journey is developing.

Included in Committed.

  • We support card payments and European bank transfers. The payment link we send you will give you the options available for your country.

  • Yes. The trial is completely free, with no payment required in advance.

  • Bundles remain valid for a generous period so families can use them at a pace that fits real life. Specific validity is shared when you confirm your bundle.

  • Sessions can be rescheduled with sufficient notice, within the validity of your bundle. Our team confirms the new time with your teacher.

  • If the match does not feel right, let us know and we will help you rematch thoughtfully. We only want your child learning with someone who truly fits.
