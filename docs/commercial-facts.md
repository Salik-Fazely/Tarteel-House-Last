# Commercial Facts

## Audience

- Children aged 5–16
- One-to-one online Quran lessons

## Languages

- English
- Arabic
- Turkish
- Persian/Farsi

## Lessons

- Free trial lesson: 40 minutes
- Paid lessons: 40 minutes
- Lessons normally continue with the same dedicated teacher, subject to availability and unavoidable changes

## Packages

- 6 lessons: €120
- 12 lessons: €220
- 25 lessons: €400
- Each package is valid for one year

## Make-up rules

- The 6-lesson package has no allowance for an absence without sufficient notice.
- The 12- and 25-lesson packages include up to four make-up lessons for absences without sufficient notice.
- If the parent gives at least 24 hours’ notice, the lesson is rescheduled outside the four-lesson allowance.
- If the teacher or Tarteel House cancels, the lesson is rescheduled outside the allowance.
- After the allowance is exhausted, an absence without sufficient notice counts as a used lesson.

## Progress and completion

- Parent progress review every two months
- Preferred format: a 15-minute parent review
- A visual WhatsApp progress report may be used when a meeting is not practical
- A certificate is issued only when the child has completed at least 30 lessons, completed an identifiable learning stage, and the teacher has confirmed completion of that stage. Purchasing a package alone does not qualify for a certificate.

## Contact and response

- WhatsApp is the preferred pre-booking question route
- Families are normally contacted within two days
- This is not a guaranteed service-level deadline

## Safeguarding

- Scheduling and service communication are managed through a parent or guardian
- Approved lesson platforms normally include Zoom or Google Meet
- Lessons are not recorded by Tarteel House by default
- Private child contact requires prior parent or guardian permission and involvement
